What we believe

Our values

Confidentiality is sacred

Your church, your pastor, and your team cannot see what you share here. Full stop. Sanctuary is your space, not theirs.

Theological integrity matters

We don't bolt Bible verses onto secular wellbeing content. Our platform is built from within the Protestant tradition, respecting its diversity and depth.

Purpose alone is not enough

Knowing your calling matters. So does joy, rest, friendship, and the freedom to be honest. Sanctuary addresses all of it.

Professional care is not weakness

We actively normalise and facilitate access to qualified psychologists — because sometimes what you need is more than encouragement.

The psalms of lament are our model

Honest, raw, held within covenant relationship. The tradition that gave us Psalm 22 gives us everything we need to build this platform.

Counsellor Referral

Sometimes you need more
than a conversation.

A warm, supported pathway to qualified psychologists who understand faith, ministry, and the specific pressures of worship leadership.

The pathway

You won't be dropped into a directory.

💬
1. Share what you're carrying
Complete a short, private intake form below. This helps us understand what you're facing and what kind of support would help most.
🔍
2. We find the right match
Our clinical coordinator identifies 2–3 psychologists from our network whose background fits your situation and tradition.
📋
3. Your context travels with you
With your permission, we share a brief summary so your first session begins from a place of understanding, not explanation.
🌿
4. Subsidised sessions
Flourish subscribers receive subsidised rates. Sessions delivered via telehealth across the US, UK, Canada, and Australia.
Request a referral

Completely confidential. Takes about 4 minutes. We respond within 2 business days.

Your information is never shared with your church, employer, or denomination. Used only to match you with an appropriate psychologist.

🌿

We've received your request.

Thank you for taking this step. Someone from the Sanctuary team will be in touch within 2 business days with your matched psychologist options.

In the meantime, your AI companion is available anytime you need it.

If you are in immediate crisis

Please contact a crisis service. US: 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988). Australia: Lifeline 13 11 14. UK: Samaritans 116 123. Canada: 1-833-456-4566.
